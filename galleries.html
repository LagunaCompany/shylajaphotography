Architecture

In this collection, I have tried to capture a few colourful images of interesting buildings and structures. I am particularly interested in the architectural heritage of India, contributed by different dynasties that ruled this vast country. During my recent visit to Agra and Jaipur in India, I was captivated by the elegance of the forts and palaces, and the unique designs and vivid colours used in their decoration. Hope you enjoy these images.

People

I enjoy taking pictures of ordinary people going on about their daily life, especially during visits to my homeland, India. Although, daily life is harsh for poor people, there is always a smile on their faces and great generosity in them. I am grateful to them for allowing me to photograph them.

Landscapes & Cityscapes

Whenever I travel, I try to capture the beautiful landscapes and cityscapes around me. In this collection, I have selected a few images of different parts of the world. I am particularly interested in taking aerial shots of the landscape while flying. I hope you will enjoy viewing them as much as I did photographing them.

Animal Life

This collection contains images of animal life in the wild as well as in captivity. It is always fascinating to observe them, especially the monkey families, as to how they relate to one another and care for one another within the group. The Macaques were taken on the Western Ghats mountain range in Kerala, where I spent hours watching them.
