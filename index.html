Welcome to my photographic website.

I do hope you enjoy this collection of photographs I have placed on this site.

I have been a keen amateur photographer for quite a number of years and have taken lots of pictures during my travels to various parts of the world. Although in the past I have used film cameras, recently I have been using digital for most of my work.

Some of my favourite subjects are architecture, human life, animal life, landscapes and plant life. As you may observe from these photos, I am especially interested in Indian architecture and landscapes.
